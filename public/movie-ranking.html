The Best Movies According to Santiago

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Godfather

Francis Coppola's all time mafia classic that sucks you into the underworld and its workings

The Irishman

A bioepic on the life of Fran Sheeran, with a special appearance from Jimmy Hoffa and his life

Gladiator

A classic story of the pursuit of revenge in classical Roman times
